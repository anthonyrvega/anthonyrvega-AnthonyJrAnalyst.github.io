Bio

“O brave new world, that has such people in it!” ― Aldous Huxley, Brave New World

My academic background is a Bachelors in Pharmaceutical Chemistry at the University of Davis, California. Since 2016, I have been working on experimental data analysis in the laboratory sector in several departments at the UC Davis Campus. During that time, I have been a Mathematical and Physical Sciences (MURPPS) Fellow in the Undergraduate Research Department, and a research intern at the Global Hub Center in Taipei, Taiwan. My areas of expertise are in PCR and ELISA plate experimentation and data analysis however I have discovered my true passion in data analytics and have gone returned to school for Data Sciences.

My passion projects include street taking photography and strength training at the gym. I also love international travel to enjoy my true passion in gastronomics.(China, Taiwan, France, Italy, Amsterdam, London, Germany, and Spain)
